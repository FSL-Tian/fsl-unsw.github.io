Introduction

The Flow Science Lab (FSL) is a group in the School of Engineering and Technology, University of New South Wales Canberra which is the Canberra campus of The University of New South Wales and is located at the Australian Defence Force Academy in Canberra. In general, FSL works on Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and its applications. The recent aim is to develop CFD tools for multi-process interactions (e.g. thermal, acoustic and mechano-chemical fluid-structure interactions) and complex flows and promote their applications in laryngeal aerodynamics & vocal-fold vibration, energy harvest, fish swimming & insect flight, blood flow & blood cells, sport-related flows, aerodynamics of aircraft, and non-Newtonian flows.

This web provides FSL News, Research Information, Members, Publications, and Links & Jobs. Please contact A/Prof Fangbao Tian if you would like additional information.
